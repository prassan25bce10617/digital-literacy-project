# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

# DO’s:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful profile.
2. Think before posting any content online.
3. Use privacy settings to protect your personal information.
4. Share useful, positive, and informative content.
5. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.

# DON’Ts:
1. Do not share personal details like passwords or sensitive information.
2. Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
3. Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
4. Avoid oversharing your personal life publicly.
5. Do not spread rumors or unverified information.
